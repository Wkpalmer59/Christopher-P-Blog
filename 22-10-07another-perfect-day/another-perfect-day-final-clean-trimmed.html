Another Perfect Day

October 7, 2022

Today was a long day, shoved off from Atlantic City at 5:40am and tied up in Norfolk VA at 6:45pm. We couldn't have asked for better weather. We are now at mile 0 (Buoy above marks mile 0) of the waterway and our off shore runs are behind us. Now we can take it easy in the waterway to Myrtle Beach.

Looking forward to prime rib in Coinjock tomorrow! :-)
