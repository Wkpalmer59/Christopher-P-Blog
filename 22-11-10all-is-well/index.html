All is Well

We spent the night in a hotel last night and I was able to monitor the boat via the camera I have on the mast. The internet actually stayed up all night and it got pretty ugly around 4am. (that's right I didn't get much sleep). We got back to the boat around 10:30 and found everything in order. No damage to be found and only a couple of minor water leaks in the cabin.

We will spend the next few days putting the bridge enclosure back up and getting everything back in order. I think we really dodged a bullet on this one.

Thank you for all your thoughts and prayers!
