Islamorada, FL

Hi All,

We cruised the inland waterway down to Islamorada yesterday. Beautiful ride with crystal clear water. A little stressful as this is the most shallow part of the inland waterway; we got a little tired of listening to the depth alarm.

We got in the middle of some kind of power boat race and had them all passing us, some very closely at very high speed and with a lot of noise. We passed the restaurant where they gathered for lunch and then a little later they passed us again! See pictures and videos below.

Once we anchored in Islamorada we took the dinghy into Lorelei's and had dinner and a few beers with our good friend John. Great to catch up with him!

We will stay here today as the wind is blowing pretty good and then tomorrow we will head down to Marathon and then on to Key West on Saturday.
