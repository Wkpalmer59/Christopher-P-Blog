Marathon, FL

Hi All

Had a relatively easy short run in Hawk Channel today from Islamorada down to Marathon. It was a little rocky and rolly but it laid down nice by the end of the day. Found a decent place to anchor in a very tight Marathon harbor, but the wind is calm so it should be a quiet night. A little on the warm side (I know, wah, wah). Caught another great sunset.
