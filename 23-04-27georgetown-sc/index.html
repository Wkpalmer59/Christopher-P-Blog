Georgetown, SC

We left Charleston this morning about 8am and passed the largest power catamaran I have ever seen, 80 feet! It was a good day with no "incidents". We arrived in Georgetown about 3pm where we will spend two nights. Weather tomorrow is going to be rainy and windy, so we decided to stay put.

Along the way we passed one of the more interesting "bridges" on the waterway. This is fairly new and is rarely used. I have passed through here many times and have never seen it across the canal.

They use the small motor on the barge to swing it across the canal and then drop the ramps so cars can drive over it. It connects the Yawkey-South Island Reserve to the mainland.

We spotted a "Mary Kay" boat and the ever present pelicans with this one posing for us.
