Palm Beach FL

Left Stuart this morning (nice facility, picture above) and had a relatively short leisurely ride down the waterway to Palm Beach.

Stopped at a fuel barge near Lake Worth inlet (first for us), where we paid under $4 for fuel for the first time since fall of 2021.

We arrived at Palm Harbor around 1:30 PM where we are one of the smallest boats here! Tomorrow is an off shore run down to Miami to avoid the waterway craziness through that area and then we enter the Keys!
