Queenstown, MD (Day 2)

Hi All,

We spent the day at the Hole In The Wall Camp location and I helped them turn up some wireless for a couple of rooms in the Houghton House that they will be using over the next few months.

On the way in we went up the Wye East River and past Wye island which was all part of the huge Wye Plantation. On the way out we got a good view of the plantation house and the black sheep on the lawn.

On our morning walks we got the opportunity to further explore the beautiful property that will one day be an awesome camp for the kids Hole In The Wall serves.

We also saw many of the Black Angus cows that are raised by the University of Maryland.

Yesterday our host let us use a vehicle to get some supplies and while out we stopped to eat at the Crab Deck where the Admiral had yummy crab rolls (just like a lobster roll, but with fresh crab meat).

Today we are headed further up the Chesapeake Bay to Rock Hall, MD where we will spend two days.
