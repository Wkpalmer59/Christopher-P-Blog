Rock Hall, MD

Hi All,

Yet another port that goes on our list to visit again. This is a small harbor and town, but very quaint with a New England feel. Instead of golf carts as mode of transportation there were many bikes with baskets on the handlebars available. The town was less than a mile from the marina so we opted to walk.

They even do interesting paintings on their storm drains; this pirate face & parrot is just one example

We also think we may have found our next house here; I mean come on, if we have to live on land.....

We spent an extra day here to wait out small craft warning and are glad we did, it was very pleasant last night and we took a dinghy ride today to explore the rest of the harbor.

Tomorrow we will head up the bay to the C&D Canal and will stop in Chesapeake City.

On our morning walk before leaving we passed this tribute to the Oysterman.
