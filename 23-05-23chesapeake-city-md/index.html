Chesapeake City, MD

Yesterday we stayed at a small marina in Chesapeake City which has a rich history centered around the digging of the C&D Canal.

In the early days of the canal, barges were pulled through using horses and mules.

The marina we stayed at was very busy as it was attached to a restaurant/Tiki bar featuring live music even on a Monday night...we can't imagine what it must be like on a weekend during the peak of summer!
