Manhasset Bay, NY

Hi All,

As you can see below, we were treated to a great sunset last night at anchor in Sandy Hook NJ.

This morning we got underway about 10:30am and got the incoming tide through NY Harbor and up the East River. Caught some great views of the NY City skyline and the Verrazano Narrows bridge.
