Rockland (day 2)

Hi all,

We spent some quality time in Rockland during our 2 night stay.

Yesterday we watched a parade of schooners come into the port (I counted 10), turns out it was the great schooner race: https://www.greatschoonerrace.com/great-schooner-race-history/

As usual we picked up some history on our morning walk. We learned about the sinking of the El Faro. https://downeast.com/our-towns/el-faro-memorial-rockland-maine/

And the History of Ingraham Point.

We also passed the Sail, Power and Steam museum. (which we did not have time to visit.

We did visit the light house museum, which was very cool. It is amazing how they constructed and maintained lighthouses back in the day.

Tomorrow we will head down to Christmas Cove (hopefully with a little less fog) so we can actually see some of the lighthouses.
