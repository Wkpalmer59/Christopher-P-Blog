New Bedford, MA

We left Cataumet early this morning in order to avoid the strong winds forecast for the afternoon. It was a nice ride and we got in just as the winds really started to pick up, and good thing as it is blowing pretty good now.

New Bedford is the only Harbor that we have been in that has a massive hurricane barrier to protect against storms and storm surge. They can close it up and fully protect the harbor from a storm surge. Amazing!
