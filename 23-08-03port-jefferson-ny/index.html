Port Jefferson, NY

On Friday we traveled from Essex to Port Jeff. It was a little sloppy out on the sound but it was a following sea so it was tolerable. When we arrived we were surprised at how empty the mooring field was, but it has gradually filled in and it is party full now. Here are videos of the before and after.
