Battleship Cove (Day 2)

Today we did the tourist thing and visited “Big Mamie“ (nickname for USS Massachusetts) along with the other exhibits in Battleship Cove, including the PT Boat exhibit where I learned that PT Boats were all wood.

My Uncle Eddie (my father’s brother) served on the Massachusetts and I was able to locate his name on a plaque on the ship.

Tomorrow we are going to head across the upper part of Narragansett Bay to Greenwich Bay and Warwick Cove where we will get a slip at a marina to charge batteries and refill water.
