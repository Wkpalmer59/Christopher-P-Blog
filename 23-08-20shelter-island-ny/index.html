Shelter Island, NY

We had an uneventful ride from Mystic over to Long Island. Got a little bumpy as we traveled through The Race: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Race_Rock_Light

Hard to believe that 6-8 feet of the water in Long Island Sound flows out and back in through the race and East River twice a day, hence the reason why the currents are so strong and can create some nasty conditions. Fortunately it was fairly calm today and we caught it close to slack tide.

We planned to stay here at anchor another night but may move on if the weather forecast changes.
