Three Mile Harbor (Day 3)

We have been sitting on the hook here in Three Mile Harbor since Wednesday. We found a good dinghy dock in the harbor where we could get ice and go for walks. There is even a small market that is quite a busy morning stop with many locals getting coffee & breakfast.

This morning we came across a beautiful old yacht that was built in 1937. It is amazingly well maintained. You can check out some more photos here: https://www.yatco.com/yachts/flying-lady-trumpy-1937-206083/

We had a stormy day yesterday so we just hunkered down in the cabin and played parlor games, enjoyed cocktails and binge watched Lincoln Lawyer on Netflix.

Today is a beautiful day so we took the dinghy over to the north end of the harbor and took a walk on the beach.

Tomorrow we will head over to Greenport and get a slip in a marina for two days so we can fill our water tanks, give the batteries a good charge, and re-supply.
