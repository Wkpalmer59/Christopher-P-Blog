Havre de Grace, MD

We arrived in Havre de Grace on Friday after a long day with the current against us in the Delaware Bay and C&D canal. We were looking for a new and interesting place to sit out some nasty weather and we found it. This is a great town with lots of history and things to do.

The town was also burned down by the British during the revolutionary war in 1777.

The weather through Monday is not great so we plan to stay here until, hopefully, Tuesday when we will head down to the Hole in the Wall Maryland camp.
