Hole in the Wall Gang Camp, Queenstown, MD

Hi All,

Just getting to our latest post this morning due to a lack of internet access during our stay at Hole in the Wall.

We departed Havre de Grace on Tuesday and enjoyed a beautiful sunrise.

We arrived at the dock at the camp around 3pm which is about 8 miles up the Wye East River. It is a nice quiet and secluded spot.

I spent Wednesday doing a walk through at the camp to gather info for the planned phone deployment.

We departed early this morning with a bon voyage at the dock from Pete and Paul and are currently headed for the Solomons.
