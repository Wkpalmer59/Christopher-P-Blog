Coinjock, NC

Yesterday we left Hampton and entered the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway passing the Mile 0 buoy in Norfolk.

We then went through the lock in Great Bridge, VA Which dropped us a couple feet.

We arrived in Coinjock around 4pm and enjoyed a delicious prime rib dinner.

Today we are headed across the Albemarle Sound and will drop anchor in the Alligatoer River.
