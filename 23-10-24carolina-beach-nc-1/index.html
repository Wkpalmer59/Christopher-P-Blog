Carolina Beach, NC

We found this mooring field on the way north in the spring and decided to spend two nights here. The dinghy dock is close and it is only two short blocks to a beautiful beach where we can take a walk in the morning and the Admiral can get her beach fix.

We found out from one of our fellow boat neighbors that there is a pier up the beach with a tiki bar, so we took an after lunch stroll there from the dinghy dock and enjoyed a couple of cocktails.
