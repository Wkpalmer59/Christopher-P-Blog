Cocoa, FL - Rocket Launch

One of the perks of our spot here in Cocoa is that we have a pretty good view of the rocket launches from Cape Canaveral. This particular launch was a Space-X Falcon 9 with a classified payload of satellites for the USSF. This one was fun because the booster lands back at the base rather than out on a drone ship so we can see it come back down and we also get a sonic boom.
