Leaving The Keys

We originally planned to stay down in the keys for another few days, but after checking the weather we saw that a storm is coming through over the weekend and will bring a lot of wind and rain, so we decided to head back early and get into the waterway north of Miami before the storm comes through.

Although we didn't get a chance to go snorkeling, we did enjoy several quiet days at anchor enjoying the sunsets and beautiful water. To celebrate St. Patrick's day we anchored off the Caribbean Club and took the dinghy in to have a couple of drinks and enjoy a great band with an awesome singer.
