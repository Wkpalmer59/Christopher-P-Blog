Silver Glen Springs

We had a comfortable cruise to Silver Glen Springs after departing Palatka. We had a few interesting sightings along the way…

We found a nice spot just outside of the springs to anchor for the night & took the dinghy in to explore…we regret not taking a photo of the hoards of boats & people who were there on a Sunday, it was reminiscent of a music festival! We decided to head back to our peaceful anchorage & try again on Monday.

Turned out to be an excellent choice; what a difference a day makes! This morning was so much more peaceful with only a few boats passing through & a few just hanging out. We were disappointed that there wasn’t much wildlife to see other than turtles & fish but after seeing yesterday’s party scene, it’s no wonder the wildlife makes itself scarce! As one of Floridas purest & cleanest springs, it’s hard to imagine 65 million gallons of water are being discharged each day but as you can see, it makes for a pretty clear spring.

Tomorrow we will work our way back to Palatka where we plan to explore a nature trail we realized we missed.
