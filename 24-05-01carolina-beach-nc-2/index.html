Carolina Beach, NC

We stayed on a mooring in Carolina Beach Sunday & Monday night. Other than dealing with a lot of wind, it was a very relaxing stay. We walked into town, checked out the boardwalk, walked the beach many times & stopped at the tiki bar on the pier.

Yesterday we traveled to an anchorage near New River Inlet. Along the way we got stuck behind a dredge with its 4 barges and piping equipment. We stayed behind it as it traversed its way through a bridge which was quite a challenge to say the least!
