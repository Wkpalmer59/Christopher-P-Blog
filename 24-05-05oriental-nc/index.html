Oriental, NC

This was a new stop for us and we were pleasantly surprised. While it was a small town, the harbor was beautiful and the marina had a lot to offer including a tiki bar and pool.

We walked to a local brewery that had live music and some great beers. The setup was kind of cool. They served beers from the bar through a window to the folks in the outside beer garden who were enjoying the music. It was definitely a local favorite as we noticed most people brought folding chairs & food, then got in line for a beer, sat down and ate while enjoying the music.

We stayed two nights, giving us time to explore the downtown area, hang out and have some down time and also enjoy a nice lunch at the Toucan restaurant located in the marina. We ended the day planning future ventures with boat buddies as we head up into the Chesapeake Bay.

Today we move on to Bellhaven, NC.
