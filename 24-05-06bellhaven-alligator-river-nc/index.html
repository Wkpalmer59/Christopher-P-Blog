Bellhaven & Alligator River, NC

We left Oriental yesterday heading for Bellhaven and encountered unexpected weather. It was so hot so we had the bridge all opened up. A few hours in, squalls kept kicking up, making us open, close, open, close, either really hot or getting soaked! The waves were hitting us broadside as we arrived in Bellhaven yesterday with the wind blowing way harder than predicted. The marina in Bellhaven is very exposed to the southwest, which of course was the direction the wind was coming from, so we had to deal with wind and waves while docking. Us along with buddy boat both made it in without any damage, so we will chalk that up as a win.

We celebrated Cinco De Mayo on board & offered to boat sit Seas The Moment (right next door) during wild wind wave weather so Dennis & Colleen could head into town to celebrate at a local Mexican restaurant.
