Hampton, VA

On Friday we moved from Portsmouth to Hampton, which was a whole 12 nautical miles, but along the way we passed the naval base in Norfolk.

Turns out it was the aircraft carrier Harry S. Truman, which is a Nimitz class carrier. They also called us to make sure we stayed clear. To be clear, Christopher P was directly hailed over the radio to maintain our course.
