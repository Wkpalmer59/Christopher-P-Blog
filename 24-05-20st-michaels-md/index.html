St. Michaels, MD

We arrived in St. Michaels on Saturday and were immediately sorry that we did not book two days there. It is a great little town with quaint little streets and homes, several restaurants, tons of shops, a brewery, winery and rum distillery. There is also a maritime museum.

Of course our first stop was the distillery and brewery where we sampled and purchased some of their wares.

We had dinner at a local saloon that was recommended by the woman in the distillery and it turned out to be just our kind of place.

In the morning we had some time to walk through the museum grounds where we spotted this interesting figurehead.

After breakfast we strolled through the town & did a little shopping before our departure since we had a short trip to our next destination, Rock Hall MD.
