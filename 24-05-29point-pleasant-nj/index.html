Point Pleasant, NJ

We anchored last night in Point Pleasant after what turned into a very windy day.

After an early morning shove off, we traversed the Point Pleasant canal and out the Manasquan Inlet into the Atlantic Ocean.

Fortunately the wind died down overnight, so as of now we have calm seas off the Jersey coast. We plan to make it through New York Harbor and up into Long Island Sound.
