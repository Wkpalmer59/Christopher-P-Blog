Campbellford, ON - Trent-Severn Day 2

We spent last night at the bottom of lock 7 in Glen Ross at the lock wall, which was very peaceful.

As we settled in last night we heard some splashing and a lot of honking and noticed a family of Canada geese were trying to make their way from the top of the lock down to the lower waterway. The parents went first and they honked like crazy trying to convince the youngsters to jump...so funny watching them look at the jump from the edge and go back to the level they are at. Since they do have the ability to fly, we wondered why they basically do a bridge jump and plop into the water.
