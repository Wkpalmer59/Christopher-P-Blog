Midland, ON - The Georgian Bay

After the hold up at the Big Chute yesterday we moved from the free dock to the blue line dock at 5:30pm so we were positioned for passage in the morning. As we touched on previously, every lock has a designated wall or dock that is painted blue, and when you tie up there you are signaling that you are ready to go through on the next available lock.

While on the free dock yesterday, one of our fellow loopers took a great drone shot of us sitting at the dock.
