Midland, ON - Day 2

We stayed 2 days in Midland to avoid the wind on Thursday since we needed to cross the Georgian Bay. We did a bit more exploring and got a few more pictures. This September, Midland will celebrate their art by having their first mural festival. This is a great spot with a nice marina, although we did have some technical difficulties with their shore power which was ultimately solved by a boater with local knowledge. The harbormaster then showed up to help our buddy boat get power which entailed a move to the only other spot in the marina with 50 amp power. Normally no power isn’t a big deal but we had already spent 2 nights with no power and coincidentally, the next morning our generator wouldn’t start. Captain figured out the problem, had the spare parts and fixed it pretty quickly the next morning.

Our boat buddy shared a picture they took of our boat in the Big Chute carriage that is pretty cool.

This morning we got an early start and are headed to the world famous Henry’s Restaurant.
