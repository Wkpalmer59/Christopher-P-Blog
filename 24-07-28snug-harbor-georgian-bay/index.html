Snug Harbor, Georgian Bay

Yesterday we had fairly short run up the small boat channel to Snug Harbor, where we found a quiet and beautiful anchorage.

Along the way we continued to travel through narrow channels with rocks close by and some really beautiful houses.

We got in early enough to drop the dingy and do some exploring.

We got back to the boat and chilled out and took in the view.

Here is a video taken from the aft deck.

https://youtu.be/8oMc59io0-w

We also saw some restless natives again.

While not one of the best sunsets we have seen, it was still pretty.

All was well and good until we woke up this morning and went out on the aft deck and found this.

That pile of bugs in the upper right corner of the step reminded Admiral of the old magnetic board game called Wooly Willie where you would use a magnetic pen to drag shavings to give the face beard, mustache and eyebrows...this boat was hairy! We had to use a good portion of our fresh water reserves hosing the boat off so we could crew and move on.

Today we headed to Byng Inlet where will stay in a marina and give the boat a good wash down and replenish water tanks.
