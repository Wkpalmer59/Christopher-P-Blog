Killarney, ON

We had heard and read a lot about Killarney and how great it was, but upon arriving there on Tuesday, we were not impressed. The marina was expensive and they packed us in on docks that are across the channel from the marina facilities and town. You have to take a water taxi to get to the other side or launch your dinghy. There is really nothing in the town except for one restaurant that supposedly serves the best fish and chips, a general store and a liquor store. Dennis and Colleen had the fish and chips and said they were good, but not something they would write home about, the general store was boring and the liquor store was picked pretty clean.

We did have a sighting of something rare.

We had booked two nights there, but quickly decided to move on and we are glad we did. We are now in Little Current and this is a great stop where we will spend two nights.
