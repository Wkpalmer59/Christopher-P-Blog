Leland, MI

We had a nice calm ride down to Leland from Charlevoix yesterday. We continue to be amazed by how clear the water is. At one point the Captain could see bottom and had to check the depth sounder only to find out we were in 25 feet of water!

Leland is an old fish town and has maintained some of its charm.
