Manistee, MI

We finally got out of Leland yesterday and had a decent ride down to Manistee. We dealt with some left over swells in the morning that rolled us around a bit but it eventually laid down nicely.

Like most of the seaport towns we visit, Manistee has a quaint downtown with great architecture and history.

We found a nice little pub last night for dinner and enjoyed cocktails and a great meal. This morning we did our walk on the boardwalk they have that runs along the harbor out to the harbor entrance.

Today we are headed down to Pentwater.
