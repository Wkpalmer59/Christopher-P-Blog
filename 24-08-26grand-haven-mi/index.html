Grand Haven, MI

We had a short but somewhat rocky ride down from Muskegon to Grand Haven on Friday and we stayed at a large 300 slip Safe Harbor marina for two nights.

We have to admit we are always impressed by the facilities and services provided at this chain of marinas. They had a pool, gym, club house and even playground for the kids. They also had a happy hour on Friday with free beer, wine and snacks and then had a free continental breakfast in the morning. The only downside was that it was not close to town, although we were able to dinghy over to the town marina.

On Sunday we moved to the town marina so we could better explore downtown and enjoy the nightly water fountain show.
