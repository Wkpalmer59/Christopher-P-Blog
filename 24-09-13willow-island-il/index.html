Willow Island, IL

On Wednesday night we anchored in a quiet anchorage behind Willow Island with 5 other boats. It was very peaceful and we didn’t wake up to any bugs!

Yesterday we left the Illinois River and joined the mighty Mississippi River. We traveled down to Alton, IL which is just north of St. Louis. We will spend 6 days here so we can get a chance to see St. Louis (there aren’t any marinas in downtown St. Louis) and we also need to slow our roll a little to remain on our loose schedule. Our boat buddies have some friends from the area that we have some weekend plans with.
