Kimmswick, MO

We left Alton yesterday and traveled down the Mississippi past St. Louis and the arch. It was cool to see the arch from the water and get our picture taken.

After passing through St. Louis we stopped at one of the less glamorous but infamous stops. Hoppie’s Marine Service is nothing but a couple of barges tied together and anchored near shore with a ramp to get to shore. They do have power, water and fuel and it is the only stop for a long stretch, so we pretty much had to stop there.

This morning we walked through the small but interesting town.

We also walked past the Anheuser estate and yes, Fred’s great grandfather was the founder of the Anheuser brewing company.

Between here and Paducah there aren’t any marinas, so our options are somewhat limited. Today we are headed for a free wall at a lock.
