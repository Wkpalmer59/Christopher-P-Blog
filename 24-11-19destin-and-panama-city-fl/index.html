Destin and Panama City, FL

On Sunday we traveled to Destin for a quick overnight as we continue to move towards Carabelle. We did catch a nice sunset at the Bluewater Bay Marina.

On Monday we did a rather long day to Panama City to a well protected marina where we are staying for two nights to wait out a cold front and the remnants of Sara.

On the way we traveled through a 15 mile man made canal, which was pretty boring, but we did run across an interesting sight.

We also came across two navy escort vessels that seemed to be protecting some kind of experimental vessel.

This morning we got laundry done before the heavy rains set in and then we hunkered down for the afternoon and evening.

Tomorrow we will continue on to Apalachicola.
